# Privacy and Safety Principles

## Principles

- No ads
- No behavioral tracking
- No selling user data
- No required account for MVP
- Local-first storage when possible
- No public profiles
- No child photos uploaded to a server in the MVP
- No medical claims
- No AI-generated interpretation of what a child “means”

## Safety Position

This app is intended as an educational and communication-support prototype. It is not a medical device, diagnostic tool, therapy replacement, or substitute for an AAC evaluation by a qualified speech-language professional.

## Data Minimization

The MVP should avoid collecting:

- Child full names
- Birthdates
- Diagnosis details
- Location
- Audio recordings
- Message history
- Photos
