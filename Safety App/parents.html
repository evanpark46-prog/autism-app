Tentative curriculum & teaching activities

TopicSuggested offline activity
Someone I Don't Know Role-play a few "someone I don't know" scenarios at home with a trusted adult playing different roles, then measure out an arm's length together so the distance is concrete.
Crossing the Street Safely Practice the look-left, look-right, look-left-again routine on a driveway or empty lot before trying it together at a real crosswalk.
If I Get Separated Agree on a "meeting spot" before entering any store, and rehearse saying the help phrase out loud a few times so it's ready if needed.
